This badge explores the basic concepts of using or creating systems that perceive or deduce information, retain it as knowledge and apply it to making decisions. 
Artificial intelligence is a broad field encompassing a wide range of technologies that are useful for building such systems. Some examples include Machine learning (ML), 
Computer vision (CV), Natural language processing (NLP), and Search and optimization.

This badge will be most beneficial for you if you understand the key concepts and are comfortable in using existing solutions to support discussions of the results related to this area.

Objectives>
(1) Identify opportunities to apply artificial intelligence to solve business problems
(2) Recommend artificial intelligence approaches for client situations
(3) Prepare for and participate in client discussions about artificial intelligence
